About us

We are a team of software developers and engineers who specialize in creating cutting-edge visualization tools for electric vehicle (EV) charge and range analysis. Our mission is to provide EV drivers and fleet operators with the tools they need to make informed decisions about their charging and driving habits, ultimately helping to reduce range anxiety and increase confidence in EV adoption.

Our Visualization Tool

Our visualization tool is designed to help EV drivers and fleet operators better understand their charging and driving patterns, as well as predict their range based on those patterns. With this tool, users can input data on their EV make and model, charging infrastructure, daily driving habits, and more. The tool then analyzes this data and presents users with a range of helpful visualizations, including: Charging Patterns: This visualization provides users with a detailed breakdown of their charging patterns, showing when and where they typically charge their vehicle, as well as how long it takes to charge. Driving Habits: This visualization shows users how far they typically drive each day, as well as how often they need to charge their vehicle to meet their daily driving needs. Range Prediction: Using data on charging and driving habits, our tool is able to predict the range of the user's vehicle on any given day, helping to reduce range anxiety and increase confidence in EV adoption. Infrastructure Analysis: This visualization provides users with information on charging infrastructure in their area, including the location, type, and availability of charging stations. Overall, our visualization tool is designed to help EV drivers and fleet operators make informed decisions about their charging and driving habits, ultimately helping to increase the adoption of EVs and reduce carbon emissions.
